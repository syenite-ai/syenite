tool guides

  • Lending rates and risk

    lending.rates.query, lending.market.overview, lending.risk.assess. Compare Aave, Morpho, and Spark on Ethereum.

  • Lending execution

    lending.supply, lending.borrow, lending.withdraw, lending.repay. Unsigned Aave v3 and Spark calldata with ERC-20 approvals.

  • Yield opportunities

    yield.opportunities and yield.assess. Lending supply, liquid staking, vaults, savings rate, basis capture.

  • Swap and bridge

    swap.quote, swap.multi, swap.status. Optimal routes, batch quotes, cross-chain bridge tracking. 30+ chains.

  • Transaction trust layer

    tx.verify, tx.simulate, tx.guard, tx.receipt. Verify before signing, confirm after. The full pre- and post-signing pipeline.

  • Wallet balances and gas

    wallet.balances and gas.estimate. Multi-chain balances and cost estimates for swaps, bridges, and contract calls.

  • Position alerts and carry

    alerts.watch (with webhookUrl for push alerts), alerts.check, strategy.carry.screen. Health factor monitoring and positive-carry strategies.

  • Prediction markets and strategy

    prediction.trending, prediction.search, prediction.book, prediction.signals, find.strategy. Market discovery, signal detection, and composite strategy search.
